About the Church

Marwari Church serves the Christian community in Mirpurkhas, Sindh, providing a place of worship, fellowship, and spiritual growth.

Sunday Service: 8:00 AM
Prayer Meeting: Friday 6:00 PM

Rev. Damji Philip (1920–2006)

Damji Philip was among the first from the Marwari community to embrace Christianity in 1940. His faith shaped generations.

The church building was built by his youngest son, Rev. Rajan Victor.
