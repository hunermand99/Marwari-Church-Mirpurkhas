About the Church

Marwari Baptist Church, located in Marvi Town, Mirpurkhas, is a place of worship, prayer, and fellowship for the Christian community. The church was established to serve believers from the Marwari community and beyond, nurturing faith, unity, and spiritual growth.

The church stands as a living testimony of faith, perseverance, and obedience to God, continuing its mission to share the Gospel and serve the local community with love and humility.

Damji Philip (1920 – 2006)

Pioneer Marwari Christian in Sindh, Pakistan

Damji Philip was a prominent figure in the Christian community of Sindh, Pakistan. He is recognized as one of the earliest individuals from the Marwari community to embrace Christianity in the region. His conversion in 1940 marked a significant moment in the religious history of the Marwari people throughout Sindh province.

Early Life and Career

Born in 1920, Damji Philip belonged to the Marwari community, a group renowned for its deeply rooted cultural and traditional values. During the British Raj, he served in the British-era police force, a position of authority and discipline. His professional background shaped his disciplined and committed approach to community service later in life.

Religious Conversion and Faith

In 1940, while still in service, Damji Philip made the historic decision to embrace Christianity during a period of great social change in the Indian subcontinent. His courageous step of faith opened doors for future generations of his family and others in the Marwari community to explore and follow the Christian faith.

Legacy and Family

To honor Damji Philip’s spiritual legacy and steadfast faith, his youngest son, Rev. Rajan Victor, established the Damji Philip Church in Marvi Town, Mirpurkhas. The church remains an active place of worship and a symbol of dedication to God and family heritage.

Damji Philip passed away in November 2006 at the age of 86. His legacy continues through his three sons: Stephen, Anthony, and Rajan Victor. His grandson Ronald continues to preserve and document the family history and the life and service of his grandfather.

Damji Philip is remembered as a pioneer who faithfully bridged his Marwari heritage with his Christian identity, leaving a lasting impact on generations to come.
